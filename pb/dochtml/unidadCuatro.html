what time it is?
(wat taim it is)

Question:
what time it is? 12:00 pm
Answer
It's noon
(its nuun; Es mediodía)
Question:
what time it is? 1:00 pm
Answer
It's one O'clock
(its uan oclock; Es la una en punto)
Question:
what time it is? 1:05 pm
Answer
It's one oh five
(its uan ou fai; Es la una cero cinco)
Question:
what time it is? 1:13 pm
Answer
it is one thirteen
(its uan thortiin; es la una trece)
It's thirteen past one
(its thortiin past uan; son trece pasando de la una)
Question:
what time it is? 1:15 pm
Answer
It's a quarter after one
(its a quarer after uan; Es la una y cuarto)
Question:
what time it is? 1:30 pm
Answer
It's thirty past one
(its thori past one; Es la una y treinta)
It's half after one
(its jaf after uan; Es la una y media)
Question:
what time it is? 1:35 pm
Answer
It's twenty-five to two.
( its thuení fai tu thuu; Es veinticinco para las dos )
Question:
what time it is? 1:45 pm
Answer
It's a quarter to two.
(its a quarer tu thuu; Es un cuarto para las dos)
Question:
what time it is? 12:00 am
Answer
It's midnight
(its midnai; Es media noche)

what date is it?
(what deit is it)

    En inglés se coloca el mes antes del día. ejemplo: day week + mes + dia(first, second) + año(20/a19)
  • 08/16/08.
  • May 16.
  • May 16, 2008.
  • Monday, May 16, 2018.
  • May sixteenth, twenty eighteen.
Nota: El año se divide en dos 2018 (20 / 18 se dice twenty eighteen)
solo las fechas del 2000 al 2009 se dice de corrido two thousand and eight
Question:
what date is it? 2 agosto 2019
Answer
it's august the 2nd, twenty nineteen
(its oguest di sequen, thuení naitiin)
Question:
what date is it? 3 diciembre 1906
Answer
it's december the third, nineteen oh six
(its dicember di thorz, naitiin ou six)
Question:
What date is today? 20 febrero 2005
Answer
today it's february the 20th, two thousand and five
(its feebiuri di thuenief, thuu thausend eand faii)
